Ha'il, is a city in north-western Saudi Arabia. It is the capital of Ha'il Region, and has a population of about 1,200,000. Hail is the heart of authentic Arab hospitality and generosity. It is the land of famous poet Hatem Al-Ta’i, stories about whose extreme generosity dot the entire Arab world and the Asia region.
